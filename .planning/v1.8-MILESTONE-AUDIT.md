## Cross-Phase Integration Issues

### Critical: DI Token Resolution Risk

`EstimateModule` registers `{ provide: ESTIMATE_FOLLOWUP_CANCELLER, useClass: NoopEstimateFollowupCanceller }` as a local provider. In NestJS, local providers override imported module exports. This means all services in EstimateModule (EstimateEmailSender, EstimateReviser, EstimateToQuoteConverter, EstimateLostMarker, EstimateResponseHandler) may receive the no-op canceller instead of the real BullMQ canceller from EstimateFollowupsModule.

**Impact:** All follow-up cancellation could be silently swallowed in production.
**Affected requirements:** FUP-05, REV-05, LOST-02, CONV-05
